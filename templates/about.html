About

The OpenShift Origin index is the place to find and browse OpenShift Community-sourced QuickStarts and Cartridges.

The OpenShift Origin index is a complimentary service for users of OpenShift Origin-based services such as OpenShift Online.

OpenShift and the OpenShift Origin Index are Red Hat sponsored Community projects.

The Index is here to help you:

  • Discover new QuickStarts and Cartridges.
  • Add your QuickStarts and Cartridges from your projects on Github.
  • Socially share the ones you are currently enjoying with your friends!
  • Give Feedback directly to the authors on their projects.
  • Contribute and Collaborate on existing projects instead of re-inventing the wheel.

The code for this Open Source project is available here

The Index is still a work in progress, so if you don't find what you are looking for, feel free to contact us or post an issue in our github repo here
